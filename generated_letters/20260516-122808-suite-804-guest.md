# ROSEWOOD

Morning letter · May 17, 2030

Intent: Quiet Restoration

Good morning, Guest.

The fog came in while the hotel was quiet. The hills are still half withheld.

We have left the first hours open. Coffee can arrive without conversation.

The fig orchard beyond the lower lawn is just beginning this week. Mara Kito opens her ceramics studio at two. No need to decide now.

A personal note from Rosewood.
